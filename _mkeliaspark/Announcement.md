<h2>Announcement</h2>
<p></p>
<p>MK@Elias Park located at 11 Pasir Ris Street 52, Singapore 518866 has
started its operation from 2 Jan 2025, with K1 students attending morning
session from 8.00am to 12.30pm.</p>
<p>Our enrolment is all year-round and you may register your child for 2025
K1 admission to MK@Elias Park if your child is born between 2 Jan 2020
and 1 Jan 2021, both dates inclusive.</p>
<p>All applications will be placed under Waitlist and you will be notified
of the application status by MK@Elias Park.</p>
<p>Please submit your application via the FormSG or QR code for MK@Elias
Park Waitlist form below :</p>
<p>MK@Elias Park Waitlist Form :&nbsp;<a href="https://go.gov.sg/eliasparkmk-waitlist" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>https://go.gov.sg/eliasparkmk-waitlist</u></a>
</p>
