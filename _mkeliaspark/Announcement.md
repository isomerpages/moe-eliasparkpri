<h2>Announcement</h2>
<p>MOE Kindergarten @ Elias Park (MK@EP) is located at 11 Pasir Ris Street
52, Singapore 518866, and commenced operations in 2025. The kindergarten
is situated within Elias Park Primary School.</p>
<p>We aim to provide quality and affordable Kindergarten 1 (K1) and Kindergarten
2 (K2) education for Singapore Citizens (SC) and Permanent Residents (PR).</p>
<p>Our MK session runs from 8.00 a.m. to 12.30 p.m.</p>
