<h3>Leave-of-Absence Application</h3>
<p>Singaporeans who are planning to go overseas for work or related concerns
may reserve places for their children in their current school under the
Leave of Absence (LOA) Scheme.</p>
<p>Upon their return to Singapore the children can be readmitted directly
to their schools.</p>
<p>Applicants are to submit their application via<strong> <a href="https://form.gov.sg/68e4c3f24e938aed620179c9" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>this link</u></a></strong> and
the LOA fees to the school.</p>
