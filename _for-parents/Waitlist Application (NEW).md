<p>For parents who are interested to apply for a school transfer to Elias
Park Primary School, please submit your application through <a href="https://go.gov.sg/epps-waitlist" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/epps-waitlist</a>.
You can also scan the QR code to submit a waitlist application. The school
will contact you when there is a vacancy for your child.</p>
<p>&nbsp;For school transfer due to change of residential address, please
apply through MOE at&nbsp;<a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/primary/transfers" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.moe.gov.sg/primary/transfers</a>.</p>
