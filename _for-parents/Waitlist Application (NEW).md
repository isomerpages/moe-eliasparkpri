<p>For parents who are interested to apply for a school transfer to Elias
Park Primary School, please submit your application through <a href="https://go.gov.sg/epps-waitlist" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/epps-waitlist</a>.
You can also scan the QR code to submit a waitlist application.</p>
